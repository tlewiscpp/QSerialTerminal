==========================================
Getting the source and contribute changes:
==========================================
Download and install tortoisegit from here:
https://code.google.com/p/tortoisegit/wiki/Download

https://github.com/tlewiscpp/QSerialTerminal
Go to the git repository of QSerialTerminal, create an account, and login. Next click "fork" at the top-left, then click @yourusername. Now the fork is created. Next press the button "Clone or download" from the top-left, and copy the URL. 

create a new folder somewhere on your hard disk.
right-click the folder, choose "Git Clone..."
enter the copied URL into the line "URL:" and press OK, it should be something like this:
https://github.com/yourusername/QSerialTerminal.git
Now the folder "QSerialTerminal" appears.

Right-click on the folder, and choose TortoiseGit->Create branch. Now choose a fun name, for instance "devel-guide" and press OK.
Right-click on the folder, and choose TortoiseGit->Switch/Checkout. From the pull down menu, choose "devel-guide" and press OK, and the close.

When you have edited or added files, right-click on the folder, choose commit -> devel-guide.
You will be asked to enter information why you made the changes. This information is stored locally on your computer.
Next, publish the changes you have made to github by right-click, and push. You will be asked for your github password. Now the changes are transferred to your version on github.

Finally, login to github, navigate to your fork of QSerialTerminal, and click "New pull request"
Provide a meaningful summary and description, then click "Save". Now the developers are notified of your changes.

===============================
Build a stand-alone executable:
===============================
Start Qt creator, open project,  browse to QSerialTerminal\QSerialTerminal.pro and then choose open
At the left, choose Project-> choose Release from the "Edit build configuration" pull down menu.
Then choose build->run Now the program is compiled in release mode
Next select build-QSerialTerminal-Desktop_Qt_5_10_0_MinGW_32bit-Release\release\QSerialTerminal.exe and drop it on C:\Qt\5.10.0\mingw53_32\bin\windeployqt.exe Now almost all needed files are copied into the folder with QSerialTerminal.exe.
manually copy the following files from C:\Qt\5.10.0\mingw53_32\bin to build-QSerialTerminal-Desktop_Qt_5_10_0_MinGW_32bit-Release\release\ :
libgcc_s_dw2-1.dll
libwinpthread-1.dll
libstdc++-6.dll

Optional: remove the .cpp .h and .o files from build-QSerialTerminal-Desktop_Qt_5_10_0_MinGW_32bit-Release\release\
